I am delighted to share with you my latest project, completed as part of my internship at the École Polytechnique des Génies : LearNovia a comprehensive educational platform.

It is an application featuring a modern, creative, and responsive UI/UX design, carefully crafted to deliver a smooth and enjoyable user experience across all devices.

LearNovia is built around three key roles: Administrator, Teacher, and Student.

The administrator can manage users, add teachers and students, assign other administrators, send notifications, and more.

The teacher can view their courses, manage chapters and their content, monitor students, assign grades, prepare tests, and also send notifications to students

The student can purchase or enroll in courses, study chapters, take tests to validate acquired knowledge, track their progress, send messages to teachers, and of course receive important notifications.

A “forgot password” feature is also included, allowing users to recover access to their account via email.

This project was developed using React, Express, MySQL, along with other powerful technologies such as Stripe for secure payments, Nodemailer for automated emails, and Axios for communication between the front-end and back-end.
